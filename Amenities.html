KTV

Our ground floor is where our KTV is located. Having access to your own private karaoke room with screens, speakers, mood lighting, and comfy seating is what it means to have a KTV, as opposed to the more western-style karaoke that is performed on a stage in front of large crowds. Different parts of the world love KTVs because of their individualized and private nature, though often with their own regional naming practices.

In the end, it's just some good ol' fashioned singing with friends in a private retreat. Of course, KTV also provides room service for food and beverages, just like any other fantastic karaoke experience.
